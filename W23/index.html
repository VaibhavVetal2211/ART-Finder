Cultural Clubs

Cultural Club

Cultural Club puts forth students' innovative ideas at inter-collegiate cultural events by providing them with necessary social, technical, environmental, and financial support.

Achievements

  • Organizes various cultural events and competitions
  • Participates in inter-collegiate cultural festivals
  • Promotes artistic expression and cultural diversity

Pictoreal

PICTOREAL is a group of high-spirited individuals and one of the most sought-after non-technical clubs at PICT. It has published 24 vibrant magazines showcasing remarkable contributions of its members.

Achievements

  • Published 24 magazines
  • Showcases student creativity and talent
  • Provides platform for literary expression

Technical Clubs

Game Development Club

GameDevutopia is the official game development club of PICT. The club's objective is to make people aware of various aspects of game development and provide a platform for collaborative projects.

Achievements

  • Collaboration with Ubisoft
  • Won various game jams at IIIT Kottayam and IIT Madras
  • Over 90 active members

PICT Automobile Club (PAC)

PAC provides a platform for students to indulge in their passion for motorsports. Students get hands-on experience in building competitive racing EVs and compete at the national level.

Achievements

  • National level racing competitions
  • Hands-on experience in EV development
  • Interdisciplinary learning opportunities

Sports & Physical Education

Sports & Physical Education

Sports allows students to compete against skilled athletes from other colleges and universities. It emphasizes student initiative and team management, allowing participants to shape their own experience.

Achievements

  • Inter-collegiate sports competitions
  • Team sports and individual events
  • Physical fitness and wellness programs
