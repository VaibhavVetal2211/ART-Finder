Our History

Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT) was established in 1983 and has been a pioneer in computer education in India. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the most prestigious institutions in the field of computer technology.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide quality education in computer technology and engineering, fostering innovation and research while preparing students for successful careers in the industry.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution for excellence in computer technology education and research, producing industry-ready professionals who contribute to technological advancement.
